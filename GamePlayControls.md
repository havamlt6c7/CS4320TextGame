Game Play Controls

Right Button
To navigate randomly selected rooms for the quest of equipment to defeat the Man Eating Plant and Onc monster that
unknowingly exist in random rooms.

Once armor is found, SELECT UP BUTTON to acquire equipment

Once movement becomes a deadend within the room, SELECT DOWN BUTTON to exit room

Once out of the room, SELECT LEFT BUTTON to engage the Man Eating Plant that has the health rating of 8
and your personal health rating of 50.

After the selection of the LEFT BUTTON, you are presented with a new menu listing:
LEFT BUTTON ATTACK, MIDDLE BUTTON USE POTION, RIGHT BUTTON DEFEND

The selection of the LEFT BUTTON, allows you to attack the Man Eating Plant or the Onc Monster.

1. A successful attack on the Man Eating Plant, equals 1 deal and a 1 point deduction of the Man Eating Plant's 
total health from 8 down to 0.
If attack is unsucessful, the Man Eating Plant deals you a 6 point health deduction from the total of 50 to 44 points.
Attack to and from you and the Man Eating Plant goes on until the death of you or it, with the 
health points of 0 in other words.

2. A successful attck on the Onc Monster, equals 1 deal and a 1 point deduction of the Onc Monster's 
total health from 15 down to 0.
If attack is unsucessful, the Onc Monster deals you a 6 point health deduction from the total of 50 to 44 points.
Attack to and from you and the Onc Monster goes on until the death of you or it, with the 
health points of 0 in other words.

The selection of the MIDDLE BUTTON USE POTION, allows you to use your equipment gain during your equipment 
treasure hunt while in the random rooms, with a 4 point gain in health.
 
The selection of the RIGHT BUTTON DEFEND, allows you to manuever so that the Man Eating Plant or Onc would possible miss 
when they deal you a death blow.  Leaving you unharmed without any deals being dealt and health deductions to content 
with while in the heat of battle.

If manuever is unsuccessful, you are dealt a 3 point health deduction, leaving you that much closer to death.
